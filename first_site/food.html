Food

• The most famous food in Russia is ‘’borshch’’ which is a beetroot soup full of vegetables and meat which is typically served with sour cream and bread on the side.

• Another traditional food in Russian is ‘’Caviar (or ikra)’’ which is usually served as a starter with pirozhkis (small baked buns filled with vegetables or sometimes meat).

• Vodka is a popular alcoholic drink in Russia traditionally made from the distillation of fermented potatoes. Beer and tea are also widely popular.
